We want to create happy memory for you and your precious fluffy friends!

Kai'n Friends™ is a young and vibrant pet supplies brand that is founded by Scarlett Liang and established in Luxembourg.

How did our story begin?

The story all began with our little devil angel Kai, a cream colored French Bulldog. As Kai has super sensitive skin and tummy, it has given us real headache trying to find safe and assuring pet supplies for him. So one day a thought hit me: why not make the stuff by ourselves, so we can be 100% assured of all the materials being used?

This was how after a 6-month research & development & test, our first product: Kai'n Friends™ Snuffle Mat came to birth.

And our little fluffy friend loves it!:)
